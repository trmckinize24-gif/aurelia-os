### ‚öõÔ∏è Core Concepts
- **[[The Inner Citadel]]:** The concept that the reasoning mind (the _Hegemonikon_) is a fortress that cannot be breached by fire or steel, only by its own assent to false judgments.
- **[[Sympatheia]]:** The Stoic belief in the interconnectedness of all things. "What is bad for the hive is bad for the bee." This connects directly to [[Systems Thinking]].
- **[[Transience]]:** The keen awareness of entropy. Marcus constantly reminds himself that "Alexander the Great and his mule driver are both dust now," serving as a check against ego.
- **[[Prosocial Duty]]:** Unlike the Epicureans who withdrew from society, Marcus argued that humans are "social animals" evolved to work together like "rows of teeth," making civic duty a biological imperative.
