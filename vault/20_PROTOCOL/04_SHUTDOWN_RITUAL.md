## ðŸ§  System Logic
> The inspiration of designing this ritual comes from [[Cal Newport]]. When he was a guest on the [[Huberman Lab Podcast]] by [[Andrew Huberman]] he spoke about his top 3 strategies for enhancing productivity and focus. 1 of these strategies was implementing a shutdown ritual, in order to close the loop on the workday, which frees cognition for other tasks unrelated to work. I strive to be present with the people in my life and to be aware of my experience as a whole when not accomplishing tasks for the sake of my own success or enjoyment. Temperance is a virtue that is core to the AURELIA philosophy, especially the elements of the philosophy derived from my reading of [[Marcus Aurelius]] and other [[Stoicism]] material.
